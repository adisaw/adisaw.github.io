ABOUT ME

Currently, I am pursuing my Masters in Computer Science at The University of Texas at Austin. I graduated with a B.Tech degree in Computer Science from Indian Institute of Technology, Kharagpur. With a CGPA of 9.62/10, I finished 4th in my Department, out of 60 students. I did my schooling from Sharada Mandir School, Miramar, Goa. I managed a 97.1% in 10th ICSE Board Examinations and stood second in the state of Goa. I later studied for competitive examinations and did my higher secondary schooling from Dempo Higher Secondary School of Science (PACE). I secured 95.1% in 12th Boards. On securing a rank of 3712 in JEE Advance I took the opportunity to study at IIT Kharagpur. I got a branch change after my first year for exceptional academic performance to Computer Science and Engineering.
